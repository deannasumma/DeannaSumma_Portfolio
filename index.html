Deanna Summa

Staff, Product Management

Deanna is a Staff Consultant specializing in Product Management within the financial services industry. Deanna has worked on both frontend and backend products, showing her the importance for Product Owners to have a deep understanding of the technical requirements necessary to bring a product to life. She completed EY D&ET's ProductLab, a program to upskill her Product Management technical capabilities. The knowledge Deanna gained here built upon her Masters degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Binghamton University.

Product Experience

Deanna has experience in UI/UX products that directly impact the bank's digital account holding customers. She understands the importance of user experience, especially around customers accessing their financial information on a day-to-day basis. Deanna has also worked on a 100% backend product to power a bank's rewards functionality. She enjoys the challenge of marrying functional and non-functional requirements as a Product Manager, and now has more in depth knowledge around the non-functional requirements.

Digital Post-Login Bank Account Dashboard

Product Manager: Digital Banking

Deanna previously supported a leading consumer bank as a product manager within their post-login dashboard space. She owned a PEGA-powered dashboard tile that populated based on the specific customer segment logged in. The purpose of this tile was to give tips and tricks to enhance the customer's online banking experience. Deanna also owned a dashboard tile that showed the customer their assigned Financial Advisor. She worked closely with design for tile creative/copy and developed requirements and user stories to hand-off to Desktop and iOS development teams. Additionally, Deanna led post-launch testing after a specific version of the dashboard went live to a customer segment. She sat in on the live usability testing sessions with the participants to understand areas of improvement the bank can make. Deanna created research reports and presented findings to client leadership.

Realtime Offer Fulfillment

Product Analyst: Partner Rewards

Deanna served as a product analyst at a leading consumer bank to guide them in the delivery of a B2B partnership product with reusable components. She worked closely with the development team and gathered requirements to build APIs that connect the bank with their partner. These APIs powered the ability for customers to receive an electronic gift card in real time upon approval of a credit card. The release of this product led to a large increase in credit card openings.

Agile Methodologies

Scrum Master

She created the framework to execute Program Increment Planning sessions with technology and business stakeholders, created product roadmap including milestones and dependencies, and developed epics, features and user stories with the product owner to build backlog for product implementation.
